## Chain of Thought and Reasoning Model
The new model GPT-o1 released by OpenAI on November 23, 2025, introduced the concept of chain of thought. It uses reinforcement learning game methods, through rewards and penalties for the model, to train the model to generate chain of thought that meets requirements. In 2025, the AI community further discovered that reasoning models are not only powerful themselves but also excellent "teachers." DeepSeek uses the DeepSeek-R1 model to distill the Qwen model to achieve high-performance small models, breaking the ceiling of Scaling Law.

## Model Distillation
DeepSeek open-sourced and announced a series of small models distilled from the DeepSeek-R1 model, such as DeepSeek-R1-3B, DeepSeek-R1-7B, etc. These models surpass the Qwen and Llama base models in performance. When distilling small models, DeepSeek used a large amount of high-quality COT (Chain of Thought) data generated by the DeepSeek-R1 model. This data contains rich reasoning logic and knowledge, helping small models better understand and handle complex tasks. Moreover, through vertical integration of COT data, expert small models with stronger vertical professional capabilities can be achieved.

## RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
In 2025, RAG technology underwent a paradigm shift from "vector retrieval" to "knowledge association." GraphRAG (Graph-enhanced Retrieval) became mainstream. By combining knowledge graphs, models can handle cross-document global questions. Additionally, breakthroughs in Long-Context technology freed some scenarios from the trouble of extensive chunking. Embedding combined with Late Interaction and Rerank improved matching accuracy, allowing RAG to remain a low-cost, high real-time, citation-traceable solution, dominating knowledge bases.

## Evolution of Mixture of Experts (MoE)
MoE (Mixture of Experts) has become standard for all high-performance models. DeepSeek further optimized the combination of multi-head latent attention (reducing KV Cache) with MoE, achieving extremely high inference efficiency through a massive total parameter count (about 671B) with very low activated parameters (about 37B).

## MCP: The "Universal Serial Bus" of the AI Era
The Model Context Protocol (MCP) introduced by Anthropic in 2025 solved the biggest pain point in the AI toolchain: tool silos. It provided an interface standard for AI Agents, driving explosive development of the AI Agent ecosystem. MCP introduced three key capabilities:
* Resources: Allows models to read external data in a standardized way (e.g., reading local files or database records).
* Tools: Allows models to execute actions (e.g., sending an email or running a Python script locally).
* Prompts: Predefined interaction patterns passed to the model through standardized protocols.

## AI Agent
If 2024 was about exploring the possibilities of Agents, then 2025 was the year Agents truly entered the productivity stage. AI Agents are no longer just talking robots; they have evolved into complete systems with Perception, Planning, Memory, and Action capabilities.

1. Maturation of Cognitive Architecture: Reasoning-Driven
Thanks to the maturity of reasoning models (such as o1, DeepSeek-R1), Agents gained powerful "slow thinking" abilities.
* Self-Reflection: When executing tasks, Agents no longer blindly follow a single path but constantly review their outputs. If code execution errors occur, they automatically iterate based on error logs.
* Long-term Memory: Through the combination of vector databases and personalized profiles, Agents can remember your preferences for a project from three months ago.

2. From Single-Agent to Multi-Agent Systems (MAS)
In 2025, people realized that a single all-purpose Agent is often inferior to a group of specialized Agents:
* Collaborative Work: Similar to a company's operation, one Agent handles architecture design, another writes code, and another performs security audits.
* SOP Automation: Complex enterprise-level processes (such as onboarding, financial reimbursement) are broken down into standardized message passing between multiple Agents.

3. Agentic Workflow: Process as Intelligence
The Agentic Workflow concept advocated by Andrew Ng was fully validated in 2025. Rather than pursuing a larger model, through multiple iterations, tool calls, and loops, smaller models (such as the distilled DeepSeek-R1-7B) can surpass top closed-source models on specific tasks.

## From AutoGLM to Doubao AI Phone
AI is no longer just a dialog box. From Zhipu's AutoGLM to ByteDance's deeply integrated "Doubao AI Phone," the LAM (Large Action Model) concept has officially landed. Models no longer just return text via APIs; they have the ability to understand screens and simulate clicks, enabling cross-app complex operations like ordering food and hailing a taxi. This means AI hardware has officially evolved from "AI Inside" to "AI Native."

## Cursor
In 2025, Cursor became a $30 billion unicorn. It is not just an editor but also an "AI pair programming partner." By indexing the entire codebase and continuously iterating on the Composer mode, developers have shifted from "writing code" to "reviewing code." It proves that in future software development, core competitiveness will shift from syntax proficiency to system architecture and requirement definition capabilities.

## Claude Code
If Cursor is the navigator in the IDE, then Claude Code is the agent that directly parachuted into the developer's terminal (CLI) in 2025. It is no longer limited to the editor's UI; by obtaining system shell permissions, it directly executes Git commands, runs local tests, and automatically fixes build errors. It marks AI's transformation from "advisor" to "executor."

## Vibe Coding: From "Typing Code" to "Conveying Vibes"
At the end of 2025, the Vibe Coding concept, promoted by tech leaders like Andrej Karpathy, swept through the developer community. This marks the third stage of software development: from "manually writing syntax" to "AI-assisted completion," and now to simply providing "vibes" — intent is code.

## Google's Gemini Architecture
In 2025, Gemini 3 Pro demonstrated the power of a native multimodal hybrid MoE architecture. Unlike other models that use external visual encoders, Gemini achieves unified processing of audio, video, images, and text at the same neuron level. This enables the model to excel in multimodal tasks and significantly improves inference efficiency.

## 2025 DeepSeek Experimental Projects: Optical Compression Technology and Manifold-Constrained Hyperlinks
* Optical Compression Technology: Further improves the compression ratio and restoration accuracy of OCR, providing inspiration for long-context compression.
* Manifold-Constrained Hyperlinks: Introduces manifold constraints on top of hyperlinks to ensure that hyperlinks do not suffer from gradient explosion or vanishing gradients.

## 2026 Year-Opening Bombshell: Prompt Skills
At the beginning of 2026, Anthropic proposed "prompt skills," causing traditional "long prompts" to fade away. Developers no longer write complex prompts; instead, they encapsulate specific conversation content into "skills" through Q&A with AI, mounted via the MCP protocol. This further reduces the context length overhead caused by prompts. By understanding developer needs through Q&A, developers can focus more on implementing business logic rather than writing prompts.

## 2026 Outlook: The Convergence of Agentic AI and Embodied Intelligence
2026 will be a turning point for Agentic AI to move from concept to closed loop:
1. From "Dialogue" to "Delivery": AI no longer stays in the dialog box; it makes autonomous decisions based on preset goals (goal-oriented). It actively monitors progress, automatically retrieves documents when encountering errors, corrects mistakes, and delivers final results.
2. Scaling of Swarm Intelligence (MAS): Tens of thousands of micro-Agents will collaborate in private networks, forming an automated "virtual factory" that handles everything from supply chain optimization to automated marketing.
3. Explosion of AI Native Hardware: With the maturity of LAM (Large Action Model), in 2026 we will see more wearable devices that do not rely on screens and are centered on voice and visual sensing. AI officially transitions from "Inside" to "Native."
4. Dawn of Embodied Intelligence: Injecting multimodal large models into robot bodies, AI will gain intuition about the physical world, truly entering laboratories and factories.
