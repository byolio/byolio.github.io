## Chain of Thought and Reasoning Models
Introduced by OpenAI on November 23, 2025, the new model GPT-o1 incorporates the concept of Chain of Thought. It uses reinforcement learning game methods, training the model to generate compliant chains of thought through rewards and penalties. In 2025, the AI community discovered that reasoning models are not only powerful themselves but also serve as excellent "teachers." DeepSeek, for instance, used the DeepSeek-R1 model to distill the Qwen model, achieving high-performance small models and breaking the ceiling of Scaling Law.

## Model Distillation
DeepSeek open-sourced and released a series of small models distilled from the DeepSeek-R1 model, such as DeepSeek-R1-3B and DeepSeek-R1-7B. These models outperform the Qwen and Llama base models in performance. During distillation, DeepSeek used a large amount of high-quality Chain of Thought (COT) data generated by the DeepSeek-R1 model. This data contains rich reasoning logic and knowledge, helping small models better understand and handle complex tasks. By vertically integrating COT data, expert small models with stronger vertical professional capabilities can be created.

## RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
In 2025, RAG technology underwent a paradigm shift from "vector retrieval" to "knowledge association." GraphRAG (Graph-Enhanced Retrieval) became mainstream. By combining knowledge graphs, models can handle cross-document global questions. Additionally, breakthroughs in Long-Context technology freed some scenarios from the hassle of extensive chunking. Embedding, combined with Late Interaction and Rerank, improved matching accuracy, keeping RAG as a low-cost, high-real-time, citation-traceable solution, dominating knowledge bases.

## Evolution of Mixture of Experts (MoE)
MoE (Mixture of Experts) has become standard for all high-performance models. DeepSeek further optimized the combination of Multi-Head Latent Attention (reducing KV Cache) with MoE. By leveraging an ultra-large total parameter count (approximately 671B) with a very low activation parameter count (approximately 37B), it achieved extremely high inference efficiency.

## MCP: The "Universal Serial Bus" of the AI Era
Introduced by Anthropic in 2025, the Model Context Protocol (MCP) solved the biggest pain point in the AI toolchain: tool silos. It provides an interface standard for AI Agents, driving explosive growth in the AI Agent ecosystem. MCP introduces three key capabilities:
* **Resources**: Allows models to read external data in a standard way (e.g., reading local files or database records).
* **Tools**: Allows models to execute actions (e.g., sending an email or running a Python script locally).
* **Prompts**: Predefined interaction patterns passed to the model via standardized protocols.

## AI Agents
If 2024 was about exploring the possibilities of Agents, 2025 is the year Agents truly entered the production stage. AI Agents are no longer just talking robots; they have evolved into complete systems with Perception, Planning, Memory, and Action capabilities.
1. **Mature Cognitive Architecture: Reasoning-Driven**
Thanks to the maturity of reasoning models (e.g., o1, DeepSeek-R1), Agents gained powerful "slow thinking" abilities.
* **Self-Reflection**: When executing tasks, Agents no longer blindly follow a single path but constantly review their outputs. If code execution fails, they automatically iterate based on error logs.
* **Long-term Memory**: By combining vector databases with personalized profiles, Agents can remember your preferences for a project from three months ago.

2. **From Single-Agent to Multi-Agent Systems (MAS)**
In 2025, it became clear that a single all-powerful Agent is often less effective than a group of specialized Agents:
* **Collaborative Work**: Similar to a company's operation, one Agent handles architecture design, another writes code, and another performs security audits.
* **SOP Automation**: Complex enterprise processes (e.g., onboarding, expense reimbursement) are broken down into standardized message passing between multiple Agents.

3. **Agentic Workflow: Process as Intelligence**
The Agentic Workflow concept advocated by Andrew Ng was fully validated in 2025. Instead of pursuing larger models, smaller models (e.g., distilled DeepSeek-R1-7B) can surpass top-tier closed-source models on specific tasks through multiple iterations, tool calls, and loops.

## From AutoGLM to Doubao AI Phone
AI is no longer just a dialog box. From Zhipu's AutoGLM to ByteDance's deeply integrated "Doubao AI Phone," the LAM (Large Action Model) concept has officially landed. Models no longer just return text via APIs; they have screen understanding and simulated click capabilities, enabling cross-app operations like ordering food and hailing a taxi. This marks the evolution of AI hardware from "AI Inside" to "AI Native."

## Cursor
In 2025, Cursor became a $30 billion unicorn. It is not just an editor but also an "AI pair programming partner." By indexing the entire codebase and continuously iterating on the Composer mode, developers shifted from "writing code" to "reviewing code." It proved that in future software development, core competitiveness will move from syntax proficiency to system architecture and requirement definition capabilities.

## Claude Code
If Cursor is the navigator in the IDE, Claude Code is the agent that directly landed in the developer's terminal (CLI) in 2025. It is no longer confined to the editor's UI; by obtaining system shell permissions, it directly executes Git commands, runs local tests, and automatically fixes build errors. It marks AI's transition from "advisor" to "executor."

## Vibe Coding: From "Typing Code" to "Conveying Feelings"
At the end of 2025, the Vibe Coding concept, promoted by tech leaders like Andrej Karpathy, swept the developer community. It marks the third stage of software development: from "manually writing syntax" to "AI-assisted completion," and now to simply providing "vibes"—intent as code.

## Google's Gemini Architecture
In 2025, Gemini 3 Pro demonstrated the power of a native multimodal Mixture of Experts architecture. Unlike other models that use external vision encoders, Gemini achieves unified processing of audio, video, images, and text at the same neuron level. This enables the model to excel in multimodal tasks and significantly improves inference efficiency.

## 2025 DeepSeek Experimental Projects: Optical Compression Technology and Manifold-Constrained Hyperlinks
* **Optical Compression Technology**: Further improved the compression ratio and restoration accuracy of OCR, providing inspiration for long-context compression.
* **Manifold-Constrained Hyperlinks**: Introduced manifold constraints on top of hyperlinks to ensure that hyperlinks do not suffer from gradient explosion or vanishing gradients.

## 2026 Year-Opening Bombshell: Prompt Skills (Skillification of Prompts)
At the beginning of 2026, Anthropic proposed prompt skills, making traditional "long prompts" obsolete. Developers no longer write complex prompts; instead, through Q&A, specific conversation content is encapsulated into "skills" by AI and mounted via the MCP protocol. This further reduces the length loss of context caused by prompts. By understanding developer needs through Q&A, developers can focus more on business logic implementation rather than prompt writing.

## 2026 Outlook: The Intersection of Agentic AI and Embodied Intelligence
2026 will be a turning point where Agentic AI moves from concept to closed loop:
1. **From "Conversation" to "Delivery"**: AI will no longer stay in dialog boxes but will make autonomous decisions based on preset goals (Goal-oriented). It will actively monitor progress, automatically retrieve documentation when encountering errors, correct mistakes, and deliver final results.
2. **Scaling of Swarm Intelligence (MAS)**: Tens of thousands of micro-Agents will collaborate in private networks, forming an automated "virtual factory" that handles everything from supply chain optimization to automated marketing.
3. **Explosion of AI Native Hardware**: With the maturity of LAM (Large Action Model), in 2026 we will see more wearable devices that rely on voice and visual sensing rather than screens. AI will officially transition from "Inside" to "Native."
4. **Dawn of Embodied Intelligence**: By injecting multimodal large models into robot bodies, AI will gain intuition about the physical world, truly entering laboratories and factories.
